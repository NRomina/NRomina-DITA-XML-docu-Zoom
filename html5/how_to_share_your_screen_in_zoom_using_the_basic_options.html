How to Share your Screen in Zoom Using the Basic Options

Here you will find instructions about sharing your screen in Zoom.

  1. Click on the Share Screen icon.
  2. A window opens with the different window screens (whiteboard, screen from IPhone/iPad, and the screens that are opened in your computer) you can share.
  3. Choose the screen you want to share, then hit Share.
You share your screen and provide visual aid for your presentation.
